# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Our Standards apply to all community spaces, both public and private. This includes
but is not limited to:
- GitHub repositories
- Issue trackers
- Pull requests
- Discussions
- Community chat channels
- Email communications
- In-person events

### Expected Behavior

- **Be Respectful**: Treat others with dignity and respect
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome and embrace diverse perspectives
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together to build positive relationships
- **Be Constructive**: Provide helpful, actionable feedback
- **Be Accountable**: Take responsibility for your actions and learn from mistakes

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Actively seeking to understand and value diverse perspectives
- Helping others learn and grow
- Celebrating the contributions of all community members

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered harassment and are unacceptable:

- **Harassment**: Any conduct that has the purpose or effect of
  creating an intimidating, hostile, or offensive environment
- **Discrimination**: Unfair treatment based on personal characteristics
- **Bullying**: Repeated aggressive behavior intended to hurt or intimidate
- **Retaliation**: Action taken against someone for reporting or participating
  in an investigation of harassment

Specific examples include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address,
  without explicit permission
- Threats of violence or inciting others to commit violent acts
- Disruptive behavior that interferes with others' ability to participate
- Spamming or sending unsolicited commercial content
- Spreading misinformation or rumors
- Intentionally excluding others from community activities

## Reporting

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns,
please report it by contacting our Code of Conduct Committee:

**Email**: conduct@gogidix.com
**Slack**: #conduct-help
**Private Message**: Any community maintainer

### What to Include in Your Report

To help us respond effectively, please include:

- Your contact information (if you're comfortable sharing)
- The names or descriptions of those involved
- What happened, including dates and locations
- Any supporting evidence (screenshots, links, etc.)
- Whether this is the first time this has happened

### Response Process

Our Code of Conduct Committee will:

1. **Acknowledge** receipt of your report within 24 hours
2. **Review** the report thoroughly and privately
3. **Respond** with an action plan within 5 business days
4. **Follow up** with you throughout the process
5. **Take appropriate action** to address the situation

### Confidentiality

All reports will be kept confidential to the extent possible. We will only
share information with those who need to know to address the situation.

### No Retaliation

We have a zero-tolerance policy for retaliation against anyone who makes a
good-faith report or participates in an investigation. If you experience
retaliation, please report it immediately.

## Enforcement

### Consequences

Consequences for violating the Code of Conduct may include:

- **Warning**: Private or public warning that behavior is inappropriate
- **Temporary Suspension**: Limited or no community participation for a set period
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from all community spaces
- **Legal Action**: Involvement of law enforcement for illegal activities

### Enforcement Guidelines

Our Code of Conduct Committee will consider the following when determining consequences:

- Severity of the violation
- Intent and impact of the behavior
- Previous history of similar behavior
- Contribution to the community
- Remorse and willingness to change
- Harm caused to individuals or the community

### Appeal Process

If you believe a consequence is unfair:

1. Submit an appeal to conduct-committee@gogidix.com within 7 days
2. Include all relevant information and supporting evidence
3. The appeal will be reviewed by different committee members
4. A decision will be made within 10 business days

## Community Guidelines

### Inclusive Language

We encourage the use of inclusive language that welcomes everyone:

- Use gender-neutral pronouns when appropriate
- Avoid stereotypes about groups of people
- Be mindful of cultural differences
- Ask for preferred pronouns if unsure

### Accessibility

We strive to make our community accessible to everyone:

- Provide alt text for images
- Use descriptive links
- Ensure documents are screen-reader friendly
- Offer multiple communication channels

### Conflict Resolution

When conflicts arise:

- Assume good intent
- Listen actively and empathetically
- Seek to understand before being understood
- Find common ground when possible
- Escalate to moderators if needed

## Diversity and Inclusion

### Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive community where everyone
feels welcome and valued. We recognize that:

- Diversity includes but is not limited to: race, ethnicity, gender, age,
  religion, disability, sexual orientation, socioeconomic status, and
  geographic location
- Inclusion is not just about representation, but about creating an environment
  where everyone can fully participate and contribute
- Multiple perspectives lead to better outcomes and innovation

### Initiatives

We actively work to promote diversity and inclusion through:

- Welcoming and mentoring new contributors
- Highlighting contributions from underrepresented groups
- Creating accessible meeting formats and times
- Providing translation services when needed
- Celebrating diverse cultural events and holidays

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All official Gogidix AI Services community spaces
- Community members when representing the project
- Project-related events, whether virtual or in-person
- Direct and indirect interactions with other community members

### Project Representation

Community members in official roles (maintainers, moderators, speakers, etc.)
are expected to model positive behavior and uphold these standards in all
project-related activities.
